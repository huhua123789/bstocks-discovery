# xStocks Is Now Live on BNB Chain with 50+ Tokenized Equities, with 100+ More Coming Soon

2026.4.30  •  2 min read

![Blog post image.](https://storage.ghost.io/c/f4/1b/f41b2cc7-c557-4fb7-82f5-ff223a27dcc6/content/images/2026/04/wide---2026-04-30T200100.890.png)

# TL;DR

- Over 50 tokenized U.S. equities and ETFs are now live on BNB Chain via xStocks, with 100+ more coming in the next few weeks.
- BNB Chain is one of the leading blockchains for RWAs globally, with $3.8B in distributed asset value and ~45,000 holders.
- Start trading today on PancakeSwap, CowSwap, with 1inch coming soon

BNB Chain is already one of the world's leading destinations for real-world assets. $3.8B in distributed asset value, ~45,000 holders, and a growing roster of names like BlackRock and Franklin Templeton, already live on the ecosystem. xStocks, one of the leading tokenized equities platforms, is now live on BNB Chain.

With xStocks on BNB Chain, you can now access over 50 tokenized U.S. equities with 100+ more coming and ETFs, think Apple, Tesla, NVIDIA, S&P 500 and more, tradeable 24/5 from as little as $10.

"This is about bringing capital markets to where users already live and trade," said Val Gui, General Manager of xStocks. "By deploying the industry standard for tokenized equities on BNB Chain, we're turning equities into programmable, onchain assets that can move seamlessly across ecosystems, applications and protocols. That unlocks a more open, liquid, and globally connected financial system at massive scale."

# What you can do right now

On BNB Chain, you can trade xStocks on PancakeSwap and CowSwap today, with 1inch coming soon. You can also use them as collateral in lending markets, or integrate them into structured strategies and automated yield products.

No brokerage account, no market hours. Just your wallet.

These are not simply wrappers around traditional finance. They are new primitives: assets that can move through DeFi, be put to work as collateral, and sit inside yield strategies alongside everything else onchain. That's the difference between tokenized equities that exist on paper and ones that are actually useful.

Further integrations are coming through Venus Protocol and Flux, powered by Chainlink, expanding what users can do with their positions over time.

# The Home of Real-World Assets Onchain

BNB Chain has become the destination for serious RWA activity:

- $3.8B in distributed asset value
- ~45,000 RWA holders
- $1.17B in transfer volume

(Source: [rwa.xyz](http://rwa.xyz/), as of April 29, 2026)

"BNB Chain is already the world's second largest destination for real-world assets. xStocks elevates this position by making traditional equities fully programmable inside our high-performance infrastructure, attracting new participants and fueling the capital efficiency and innovation that define BNB Chain's competitive edge," said Nina Rong, Executive Director of Growth at BNB Chain.

These numbers reflect actual usage assets being held, traded, and put to work across the ecosystem. xStocks adds a new and highly anticipated category to that picture.

The RWA stack on BNB Chain has been building steadily. [Tether Gold (XAUt)](https://www.bnbchain.org/en/blog/tether-gold-xaut-launches-on-bnb-chain), [BlackRock's BUIDL fund](https://www.bnbchain.org/en/blog/blackrocks-buidl-fund-launches-bnb-chain-tokenized-by-securitize-and-accepted-as-collateral-on-binance), [CMB International's $3.8B money market fund](https://www.bnbchain.org/en/blog/cmb-international-and-bnb-chain-bring-3-8b-money-market-fund-onchain), and [Franklin Templeton's Benji platform](https://www.bnbchain.org/en/blog/franklin-templetons-benji-technology-platform-onboards-bnb-chain-unlocking-the-next-era-of-tokenized-finance) are already live here. Tokenized equities are the next layer.

The next chapter of onchain capital markets is on BNB Chain.
